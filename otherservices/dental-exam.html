**Dental Exam & Cleaning in Manassas, VA**

Looking forward to your next dental appointment is not something that comes naturally to everyone. Most people cringe at the thought of visiting the dentist and will do almost anything to avoid it. However, regular checkups are essential for healthy gums and teeth, and a glance in the mirror or brushing your teeth twice daily does not cut it.

Dental checkups allow dentists to detect problems early on before they escalate into more significant issues and more expensive procedures. We at Chonky Dental Care believe that prevention is always better than cure. That’s why we have made affordable dental services accessible and convenient for our patients.

**Dental Cleanings**

A dental cleaning is a dental procedure that removes tartar and bacteria from the teeth and gums. The cleaning removes the buildup on your teeth, leaving them clean. Dental cleanings also help prevent gum disease and tooth decay.

Dental cleanings are an essential part of your regular oral health care routine. A dental cleaning is recommended once every six months. The more often you visit the dentist, the more likely it is that you can catch and address problems early on.

**Dental Exams**

A dental exam is a visual, hands-on, and sometimes X-ray examination of your teeth and oral cavity to check for and diagnose oral health issues. A dentist will first look at your teeth, gums, and tongue and then use dental instruments to probe, scrape, and measure your gums to get a better idea of their condition and for him/her to determine if any dental treatment is needed.

Dental exams also help determine your risk of oral diseases and identify risk factors for tooth decay and gum disease. A dental exam can help prevent many oral and dental conditions, including tooth decay and gum disease.

Early detection of oral health issues can help prevent more serious problems and even point you in the direction of a treatment plan that will keep you from needing dental surgery in the future.

**The Advantages of a Dental Exam and Cleaning**

A dental exam and cleaning are essential procedures that help detect and address many dental problems early on. A dental exam and cleaning can help find cavities, gum disease, and other problems with your oral health that you might not be able to see with the naked eye.

An exam and cleaning at the dentist can help stop tooth decay, gum disease, and even the need for crowns, dentures, and other procedures.

A dental exam and cleaning can help reduce your tooth loss risk and improve your overall general health. Regular dental checkups and cleanings are essential to your dental care routine.

**What to Expect During a Dental Exam**

A dental exam typically lasts between 15 and 60 minutes, depending on your dental health and the number and type of dental issues your dentist finds during the exam.

A dental exam begins with the dentist visually examining your teeth, gums, and tongue to assess their condition and to identify any dental issues. The dentist may then use tools like probes and scrapers to look at your teeth and gums in more detail and figure out how bad any dental problems are.

A dental exam may also include taking X-rays to better view the mouth and an accurate assessment of the type of infection you may be dealing with.

**FAQs**

Why do I need to go to the dentist every six months?

A routine dental visit can help detect and prevent small issues from becoming major problems. Regular cleanings and x-rays can help your dentist find and remove small cavities before they become larger and more difficult to treat. This saves you time, money, and a lot of discomfort.

How do regular brushing and flossing help my teeth?

Brushing your teeth twice a day is essential to maintaining healthy teeth and gums. It can help remove plaque and keep your teeth free of harmful bacteria. Regular flossing can help reduce gum disease and tooth decay.
