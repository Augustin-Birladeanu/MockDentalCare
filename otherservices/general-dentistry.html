**General Dentistry in Manassas, VA**

Let’s face it; most people don’t love going to the dentist. But if you are looking for a general dentist service provider who will provide compassionate and comprehensive care, you will be glad to hear that Chonky Dental Care offers just that and more.

General dentistry is a field of dentistry that focuses on prevention and oral health maintenance rather than the management of acute dental problems. The general dentist can also help find things that put you at risk for oral diseases, like smoking, using drugs or alcohol, not taking care of your stress, not eating well, etc.

**We are committed to your oral health**

If you are looking for a dentist who puts your oral health first, you’ve come to the right place. At Chonky Dental Care, our team is committed to your oral health. We will take the time to learn about your oral health concerns and advise you on how to keep your mouth healthy.

It’s essential to visit a general dentist for regular dental check-ups at least twice a year. A dental exam can help find early signs of gum disease, oral cancer, tooth decay, and other problems with your mouth's health.

A dental examination is essential for the early detection of oral health issues such as periodontal disease, tooth decay, or oral cancer. A dental examination can help you prevent dental disease and maintain oral health.

**We offer a wide range of general dentistry services:**

Dental Cleaning

Dental cleaning is the first step in comprehensive dental care. It’s also the most common preventive dental treatment.

Dental cleaning services include teeth cleaning, polishing, and fluoride treatments. The dentist will use special tools and specialized cleaning solutions to remove plaque, tartar, and other debris from your teeth. Dental cleaning services provide a wide range of benefits. Regular dental cleanings can significantly reduce your risk of gum disease and other problems with your mouth.

Dental Examinations

A dental examination is a vital part of general dentistry services. It’s your chance to learn about your oral health status and discuss any concerns you have about your teeth and gums.

Your general dentist will do a thorough oral exam that includes looking at your medical history and taking x-rays.

Your general dentist may recommend specific treatments depending on your oral health status. Potential treatments may include dental sealants, fluoride, or oral hygiene instructions.

Dental X-rays

Dental x-rays are an essential part of general dentistry services. They let your dentist see your teeth and other oral structures in full view. X-rays are a quick and painless procedure that doesn’t require any special preparation.

X-rays can reveal a lot of information about your oral health. Dental x-rays can find things like tooth decay, broken teeth, and swelling or infection in your gums.

Root Scaling and Planning

Root scaling and planning (also known as periodontal treatment) is an effective treatment for gum disease. It involves removing plaque and tartar from your gum line, which helps your gums heal by reducing inflammation and preventing bone loss.

If you’ve been diagnosed with gum disease, your general dentist may recommend periodontal treatment. This procedure is often done in conjunction with other general dentistry services, like scaling and polishing the teeth and using dental floss.
